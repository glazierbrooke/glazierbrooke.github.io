What is Animal-Assisted Therapy?

 Animal- Assisted Therapy is a stress reliving therapy preformed by a handler and their pet. In recent years, it has become very poular in certain environments including schools, senior homes, test centers and hospitals. Types of animals used can include birds, dogs, cats and rabbits. What's great is that all age groups of can benefit from sessions.
